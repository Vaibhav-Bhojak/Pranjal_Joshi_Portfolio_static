About The Doctor

Compassionate Care Rooted in Science

Hello, I am Dr. Pranjal Joshi. I believe that mental health is not just the absence of illness, but the presence of wellbeing. With over 15 years of clinical practice in India and abroad, my goal is to provide a sanctuary where you can speak freely without fear of judgment.

My approach combines modern pharmacological treatments with psychotherapy techniques tailored to your unique cultural and personal context. Whether you are struggling with anxiety, depression, or life transitions, we will work together to build a path toward recovery.
